About us

BORN TO
DOMINATE
THE SKIES



Shauryan Aerospace is building the next generation of autonomous aerial platforms — engineered for intelligence, built for precision, and trusted for protection.

Founded with a singular mission to advance India's indigenous UAV capability, we design, manufacture and deploy end-to-end unmanned aerial systems for defence surveillance, agricultural intelligence, disaster response, and commercial operations.

Every aircraft we build embodies three core principles: raw Power to carry meaningful payloads at altitude, Precision in navigation and AI inference, and Protection — ensuring safety of operators, assets, and mission-critical data.
